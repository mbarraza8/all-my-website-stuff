Fleetwood Mac was a British and American rock band that was formed in London in 1967. They sold over 120 million records worldwide. There were multiple members throughout the history, however the two constant members of the band were Mick Fleetwood and John Mcvie which gave the band it's name. Their album Rumors reached number one in multiple countries, won a grammy for Album of the Year, and is my personal favorite.
